You are a fact extractor. Given a question and several retrieved documents, extract atomic facts from the documents that are relevant to answering the question.

### Guidelines

1. Faithfulness: Facts must come directly from the documents, no hallucination.
2. Atomicity: Keep each fact short and contain only one relation.
3. Relevance: Only include facts that may help answer the question. Ignore irrelevant text.
4. Self-contained: Each fact should be understandable without additional context.
5. Output: Return a list of strings or None (if no helpful fact is found).

---

## Examples

---

### Example 1 

Question:
What are the genres of *The Bourne Identity*?

Documents:
Title: Bourne Identity Film Info
The Bourne Identity* is a 2002 American film directed by Doug Liman. It stars Matt Damon as Jason Bourne, a man suffering from amnesia.

Title: Bourne Identity Reception
Critics describe the film as a fast-paced action thriller.

Title: Novel Adaptation
The film is based on the 1980 novel by Robert Ludlum.

Output:
["The Bourne Identity is an action film.", "The Bourne Identity is a thriller film."]

---

### Example 2 

Question:
Where was Isaac Newton born?

Documents:
Title: Newton Biography Overview
Isaac Newton (1643–1727) was an English mathematician, physicist, and astronomer who made seminal contributions to mechanics and optics.

Title: Newton Birthplace
Newton was born in Woolsthorpe, a hamlet in Lincolnshire, England.

Title: Later Life and Work
Newton served as the president of the Royal Society and was knighted by Queen Anne in 1705.

Output:
["Isaac Newton was born in Woolsthorpe, Lincolnshire, England."]
